介绍

随着大型语言模型（LLM）的快速发展，它们在许多现有基准上已表现出饱和性能，这使得我们难以有效区分前沿模型的能力。同时，现有高难度基准往往存在学科领域狭窄、答案形式过于简化以及易受数据污染等问题。为应对这些挑战，我们推出了ATLAS (AGI-Oriented Testbed for Logical Application in Science)，一个旨在推动领域向前发展的全新评估套件。

  • 高度原创与抗污染： 所有问题均为全新创建或经过大幅改编，以防止测试数据泄露。我们通过创新的、多阶段的数据生成和验证流程确保问题的质量。
  • 跨学科焦点： ATLAS 横跨数学、物理、化学、生物、计算机科学、地球科学和材料科学七大核心领域，旨在评估模型整合知识和跨域推理的能力。
  • 高保真答案： 我们优先考虑复杂的、开放式的答案，这些答案涉及多步推理和LaTeX格式的表达式，而不是简单的多项选择，以更真实地反映科学探究。
  • 严格的质量控制： 所有问题均由博士及以上水平的领域专家手工创建，并经过多轮专家同行评审和对抗性测试，以确保其难度、科学价值和正确性。

Introduction

With the rapid development of Large Language Models (LLMs), they have demonstrated saturated performance on many existing benchmarks, making it difficult for us to effectively distinguish the capabilities of frontier models. Meanwhile, existing high-difficulty benchmarks often suffer from narrow disciplinary domains, oversimplified answer formats, and susceptibility to data contamination. To address these challenges, we introduce ATLAS (AGI-Oriented Testbed for Logical Application in Science), a novel evaluation suite designed to advance the field.

  • Highly Original & Contamination-Resistant: All questions are newly created or significantly adapted to prevent test data leakage. We ensure question quality through innovative, multi-stage data generation and validation processes.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Focus: ATLAS spans seven core domains: mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, computer science, earth science, and materials science, aiming to evaluate models' ability to integrate knowledge and perform cross-domain reasoning.
  • High-Fidelity Answers: We prioritize complex, open-ended answers involving multi-step reasoning and LaTeX-formatted expressions, rather than simple multiple choice, to more authentically reflect scientific inquiry.
  • Rigorous Quality Control: All questions are hand-crafted by domain experts at PhD level or above, and undergo multiple rounds of expert peer review and adversarial testing to ensure difficulty, scientific value, and correctness.

排行榜

关于榜单评估：所有模型的结果均由 OpenAI-o4-mini (公开集) 或 GPT-OSS-120B (私有集) 作为评判模型进行评估得出。ATLAS提供两个榜单：公开集榜单基于完全开放的数据集进行评测，任何人均可复现；私有集榜单则基于我们持有的、未公开的高难度测试集进行评测，旨在更真实地检验模型在前沿问题上的推理能力，并有效防止数据污染。

Leaderboard

About Evaluation: All model results are evaluated using OpenAI-o4-mini (public set) or GPT-OSS-120B (private set) as the judge model. ATLAS provides two leaderboards: The Public Set Leaderboard is based on completely open datasets that anyone can reproduce; The Private Set Leaderboard is based on our unreleased high-difficulty test set, designed to more authentically test models' reasoning capabilities on frontier problems and effectively prevent data contamination.
